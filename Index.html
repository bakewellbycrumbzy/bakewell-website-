About Us

Bakewell by Crumbzy is a small-batch home bakery rooted in comfort and care. Every dessert is thoughtfully crafted using quality ingredients and a patient, hands-on process that values flavour as much as feeling. Made fresh on preorder, Bakewell’s offerings are meant to be savoured slowly — familiar, honest, and quietly indulgent.
